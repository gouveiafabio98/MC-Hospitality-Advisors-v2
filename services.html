Hotel Pre-Opening & Opening Services

As part of our hotel pre-opening services, we will develop a taylor-made strategic plan to outline the unique features of your property, develop a long-term marketing strategy and train the team to deliver an authentic experience to every guest.
Consultancy services provided usually include
  • Hotel Strategy Book
  • Pre-opening budget and financial forecast for the first 5 years of the hotel operations
  • Manning guide and recruitment schedules, HR administration plans, salary structure and benefits, leave systems and the hotel HR handbook on policies and regulations
  • Hotel SOPs (Standard Operating Procedures)
  • Sales and Marketing plan
  • PMS selection
  • Test runs

Consultancy services provided usually include
  • Monthly P&L analysis
  • Monthly site visit and Audit
  • CAPEX analysis
  • Budget reviews
  • Third-party meetings

03

Hotel Asset Management

MC Hospitality Advisors monitors the performance of properties managed by third party hotel companies in most markets. We support hotel owners, banks or funds, throughout the life of their hotel assets. If required, we can act as external expert mediators in contract interpretation disputes. We supervise the hotel operators that manage the assets to make sure we extract the highest possible return and realize an adequate asset value increase for the owner.
Consultancy services provided usually include
  • Running an initial diagnosis of all the areas of the hotel and of its competitive performance.
  • Establishing an action plan to boost hotel performance.
  • Participating in the annual budget negotiation, OPEX & CAPEX level
  • Monthly activity reporting: operations + strategy + market opportunities
  • Interim Management al all operacional levels
  • Focusing on reaching the commonly agreed strategy between the hotel management company and the hotel owner is reached
